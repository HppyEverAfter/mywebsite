About Us

Learn more about Escape to Maco and our mission

Our Story

Escape to Maco was created with a simple mission: to help travelers discover the hidden gems and breathtaking locations in Maco, Davao de Oro. As locals who love our hometown, we wanted to share the beauty of Maco with visitors and make their travel experience memorable.

We understand the hassle of traveling to unfamiliar places without proper guidance. Our website aims to provide detailed information about the best spots in Maco, making your journey convenient and enjoyable.

From natural wonders to cultural sites and leisure destinations, we've curated a comprehensive guide to help you plan your perfect "laag" (trip) to Maco.

Our Mission

To help travelers explore Maco with ease by providing comprehensive information about the best destinations, promoting local tourism, and supporting community businesses.

Our Vision

To make Maco a must-visit destination in Davao de Oro by showcasing its natural beauty, cultural heritage, and unique attractions while promoting sustainable tourism practices.
