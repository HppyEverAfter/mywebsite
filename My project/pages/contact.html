Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions

What are the best times to visit Maco?

The best time to visit Maco is during the dry season from November to May. However, each season offers unique experiences, so it depends on what you want to see and do!

How do I get to Maco from Davao City?

You can reach Maco from Davao City by bus, van, or private vehicle. The journey takes approximately 1.5-2 hours depending on traffic conditions.

Are there accommodation options in Maco?

Yes, Maco offers various accommodation options ranging from budget homestays to mid-range resorts. Contact us for recommendations based on your preferences.

Can you help with tour arrangements?

Absolutely! We can connect you with local guides and help arrange tours to the best spots in Maco. Just specify your interests in the contact form above.
